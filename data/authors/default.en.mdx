## About Me

I am a frontend engineer with hands-on experience mainly in e-commerce platforms and logistics systems. I started as a junior engineer, then worked with the architecture of a large North American e-commerce platform, and later helped build a modern booking system with frontend-backend separation for a logistics product from 0 to 1 together with my teammates.

My stack evolved from early JavaScript and jQuery into TypeScript, React, Next.js, and React Native, and I have also touched some backend work in C# .NET.

Over the last one to two years, as AI has become a major shift in software development, I have also been exploring how AI can be applied in real engineering work, and I hope to keep recording those experiences and ideas on this blog.
