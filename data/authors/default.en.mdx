# About Me

I am a frontend engineer with hands-on experience mainly in e-commerce platforms and logistics systems. I started as a junior engineer, then worked with the architecture of a large North American e-commerce platform, and later helped build a modern booking system with frontend-backend separation for a logistics product from 0 to 1 together with my teammates.

My stack evolved from early JavaScript and jQuery into TypeScript, React, Next.js, and React Native. I have also worked on some backend service API implementation, including C# .NET Core.

In recent years, especially with the arrival of smarter and more reliable AI models, I have been exploring how AI can be applied to software development, and I hope to keep recording those experiences and ideas on this blog.

## What I’m Working On

- **Wild ideas and side projects**: building side projects that I used to abandon because the payoff did not justify the effort, but that I can now develop and validate quickly at much lower cost with help from AI agents.
- **Connections With Heart**: using writing to share and record how I think about technology, work, and life.
- **AI-assisted Engineering**: designing workflows so AI can support development work more effectively.
