## Background and Technical Positioning

I hold a master's degree in Information Management from National Chi Nan University. Over the last decade, I have built experience across large-scale e-commerce platforms, internal business systems, and logistics products. My main stack includes JavaScript, TypeScript, React, Next.js, React Native, C#, and .NET.

At Newegg, I worked across B2B and B2C e-commerce products, Mobile Site, and React Native App development. Over time, I also took on Frontend Leader and Staff Engineer responsibilities, coordinating work and technical decisions across teams in Taiwan, the United States, and China.

More recently, at SJ Clemenger, I led frontend architecture and development for a next-generation logistics and forwarding management system. That work combined 0-to-1 React / Next.js engineering setup, multilingual platform design, Azure CI/CD, and integration with existing OutSystems-based TMS and warehouse systems.

## What I Work On

The work I care most about usually sits at the intersection of:

- **Enterprise Frontend Architecture**: building frontend systems that can evolve safely over time
- **System Design for Real Operations**: treating frontend as part of business-critical workflows, not just UI delivery

## How I Work

I prefer to solve problems in an engineering-driven way instead of aiming only for short-term delivery.

To me, a healthy system usually requires:

- clear module boundaries and data ownership
- a codebase structure that stays understandable and refactorable
- conventions that support team collaboration and code review
- documentation and instructions that help both new teammates and AI agents get productive quickly

That is why I continue to invest in frontend architecture, engineering productivity, and technical writing.
